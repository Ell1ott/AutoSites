Philosophy

We reject the templated web. Every pixel is placed with intentionality, every easing curve hand-drawn. We build sites you can feel.

Precision

0.5px border definitions. Sub-pixel typography rendering. 120fps motion profiles. We obsess over the microscopic details that others ignore.

Tactility

Digital shouldn't mean distant. Our interfaces respond to interaction with the weight and resistance of physical materials.

Culture

Located at the intersection of high fashion and high technology. We create artifacts for brands that demand a signature presence.
